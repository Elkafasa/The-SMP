The SMP — Server Information

General Info

Minecraft Version: 1.21.11

Backups: Bi-daily (Every 48 hours)

Server Launch Date: 2026 mid-end february

End Dimension: Scheduled to unlock 2 weeks after launch

Network Structure

The server now operates as a network to ensure variety and stability:

The Lobby: The central hub and gateway to all modes.

Survival SMP: The core progression-based world.

Minigames (Coming Soon): Bedwars and Skywars are currently under development.

World & Border

Starting Border: $10,000 \times 10,000$ blocks.

Border Expansion: The world border expands through specific progression achievements. The Ender Dragon’s death serves as the primary trigger for a major expansion.

Season Resets

Resets are not automatic or scheduled. A season reset will only occur if:

Server activity drops significantly.

A majority of active players vote for a fresh start.

Combat System (Anti-Combat Log)

To ensure fair play, a custom PvP tracking system is active:

Combat Timer: Entering PvP triggers a 15-second combat window.

Visuals: A dynamic Bossbar displays your remaining combat time.

Combat Logging: Disconnecting while the combat Bossbar is active results in instant death and a server-wide broadcast of the log.

Note: Only player-vs-player damage triggers the timer. Environmental or mob damage does not count.

Gameplay Rules & Restrictions

Ender Chests & Shulkers: Unobtainable until the Ender Dragon is defeated.

Elite Weaponry: Maces and Netherite Spears are locked until the post-Dragon phase.

Voice Chat: Required. The server uses Simple Voice Chat.
Download here: https://modrinth.com/plugin/simple-voice-chat

Commands:/tpa — Request teleportation to a friend./sethome / /home — Manage your personal spawn points.
